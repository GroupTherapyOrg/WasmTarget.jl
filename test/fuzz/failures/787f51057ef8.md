## Analysis
**Root cause: two known-deep gaps compose.** `length(get(Dict(0=>"", 0=>""),
length(v), strip("héllo")))`: the key `length(v)` (an Int) is absent from the
Int-keyed Dict, so `get` returns the **default** `strip("héllo")`, and the outer
`length` then measures that. Two open roots:
1. **`strip` on multibyte UTF-8** — [[0beb5ec969a2]] (drops a byte of `é`, so the
   length is wrong); and
2. **duplicate-key `Dict` construction** — `Dict(0=>"", 0=>"")` (cf. [[dbac068444b5]]).

The wrong value flows from the strip-unicode bug via the Dict default. **Deferred to
Part 2** — closes for free once strip-unicode and Dict duplicate-key are fixed.
