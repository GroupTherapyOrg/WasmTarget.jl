## Analysis
**Root cause: a `Dict` constructed/used inside a `map` kernel traps** — the
intersection of two known-deep areas: Dict codegen and the map-kernel-dependency bug.
`length(Dict(0=>0, 0=>y))` works standalone but traps when it's the body of
`map(y->…, v)` — a compiled function pulled in as a map kernel mis-executes (same
class as [[3b005c4957f7]] length-in-map and [[5511171c8055]] atan-in-map). The Dict
construction also exercises the ref/i64 Dict codegen that needed many earlier fixes.

Shares its root with [[f3829eae0fbb]] (same `map(length(Dict(...)))` shape, key from
the closure var). **Where to fix:** the map-kernel-dependency codegen (so a compiled
Dict-using kernel runs correctly), plus Dict-in-kernel allocation. **Deferred to
Part 2** (deep codegen).
