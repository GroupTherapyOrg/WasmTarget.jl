## Analysis
**Root: scratch-local aliasing across operand boundaries (same class as the
now-fixed `maximum∘sort` cluster).** Every sub-expression is individually correct
under the harness — probed directly: `length(sort([0f0,0f0,0f0]))`=3 ✓,
`argmax([0,0,0])`=1 ✓, `min(1,2)`=1 ✓. Only the **composition** diverges
(native=4, wasm=5): one operand of the outer `+` clobbers a scratch local the
other still needs, so either the Float32-sort length or `min(argmax,…)` is read
back wrong. `length(sort([0f0,…])) + min(argmax([0,0,0]), x)` mixes a `Vector{Float32}`
producer and a `Vector{Int64}` producer feeding an arithmetic combine — the two
allocate overlapping scratch. Fix = local reuse must not alias across operands
(Part 2; see also `905344436a9f`, `a48cf6e47497`).
